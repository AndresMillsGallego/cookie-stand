Pat's Salmon Cookies

Welcome to Pat’s Salmon Cookies! We are so glad that you found us and decided to visit our site. We got our start over 30 years ago when our founder, Pat, accidentally mixed some smoked salmon into his batch of holiday cookies! Thank goodness he decided to try them before tossing them out! The rest is history, so they say, and an empire was born. After one taste Pat knew he was on to something and was quick to enlist the help of his entire family to get things going. Our first official cookie sold came from a PTA bake sale at Pat’s youngest son’s school.

From there the word spread and demand grew. Pat started to hit the local farmers markets and even rented a stall in the Pike Place Market (right next to the fish throwers as luck would have it)! Interest and demand kept growing for this unique product. It was hard to keep up at times, but Pat always wanted to stay involved and make sure the quality of his product stayed at the highest level.

Today we are lucky to be keeping the dream alive and have 5 locations (and counting) all across the globe. While Pat has since retired, rest assured that the company is in good hands. Pat’s youngest son Alexander (The same Alexander from the bake sale so many years ago), is in charge now. He and the entire staff work tirelessly each day to not only continue delivering quality Salmon Cookies, but also to make his Dad and entire family proud.
